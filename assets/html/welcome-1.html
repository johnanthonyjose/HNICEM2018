Welcome Message

Prof. Elmer P. Dadios, Ph.D.

General Chair,
IEEE HNICEM 2019

Chair, Awards & Recognition Committee,IEEE Asia-Pacific Region

EXECOM Member, IEEE Region 10

I am honored and privileged to serve as the General Chair of the 11th IEEE International Conference on Humanoid, Nanotechnology, Information Technology, Communication and Control, Environment and Management (IEEE HNICEM 2019). The IEEE HNICEM 2019 is organized by the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society, Philippines Chapter. It is one of the premier IEEE technical conferences in the Asia Pacific Region.

The IEEE HNICEM 2019 is a cross-disciplinary conference that brings together technologists, engineers, scientists, investors, and representatives from NGOs, governments, academia and various industries to promote discussions on technology developments to improve the life of the people. The theme for this year, “Global Resilience and Sustainability for Future Earth”, tackles global sustainability challenges, in particular the relationship between technology, society and the environment. This conference aims to share insights on the future of sustainability and human interaction with the planet. It reflects the relevance of IEEE HNICEM in providing a sense of direction for science, technology, society and environment for future earth.

This year, HNICEM received more than 440 technical paper submissions but only 59 percent of these were accepted. We want to ensure that only quality papers are presented in this conference. The highlights of this year’s conference are Five plenary lectures from well-known scientists; and 265 technical papers presentations categorized into interactive and parallel sessions. This diversity provides ample opportunities for the development of new ideas that are needed to meet the numerous technological, economic and environmental challenges that face future earth.

On behalf of the IEEE HNICEM 2019 organizing committee, I would like to express our deepest appreciation to the members of the International Advisory Board. We are forever grateful to you. The members of the International Advisory Board are experts in their field of endeavors and have provided help and guidance throughout the long period of planning for this conference. We are also grateful to the international scientific committee who provided assistance in reviewing and ensuring that only good quality papers are accepted in this conference.
